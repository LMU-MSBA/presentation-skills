The Power of Takeaway Titles

Before: Descriptive Title
Q4 Marketing Channel Performance

Describes what the data shows, but doesn't tell the audience what to think or do.

Forces the audience to figure out the "so what" themselves.

After: Takeaway Title
Email marketing delivers 9x more conversions per dollar than display ads

States the key finding and implies the action to take.

Audience immediately understands the insight and recommendation.
